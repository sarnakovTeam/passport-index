Global Passport Index

The Global Passport Index is composed of three standalone indices: the Enhanced Mobility Index, the Investment Index, and the Quality of Living Index.

With the Enhanced Mobility Index, we intended to go beyond a simple count of the number of countries or territories a passport gives immediate access to. We, therefore, not only assess the different levels of access a passport may provide (from unlimited visa-free access to restricted travel) but also give scores to the country or territory it is giving access to, according to its overall “attractiveness”.
